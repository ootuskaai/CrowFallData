*****職業 *****
    德魯伊 (Druid)

*****可用種族 *****

    鳥人(Fae) 半精靈(Half-Elf)  木精靈(Wood_Elf)

*****素質與裝備(Stats and Equipment)*****

        **素質(Stats)**

            智力 (Intelligence): 爆擊傷害 (Critical Damage), 治療加成 (Healing Bonus), and 攻擊力 (Attack Power)
            力量 (Strength): 傷害加成 (Damage Bonus)
            敏捷 (Dexterity): 爆擊機率 (Critical Strike) and 所有抗性 (Resist All)
            精神 (Spirit): 資源力量 (Support Power) and 耐力 (Stamina)
            體質 (Constitution): 血量 (Health)
            資源魔力 (Resource): 精華 (Essence, Max 1500)

            *support power increases you healing in the same way that attack power increases your damage.
            *支持力量增加你的治療量，就像攻擊力增加你的傷害一樣。

        **裝備(Equipment)**

            護甲 (Armor): 皮革 (Leather)
            武器 (Weapons): 鐮刀 (Sickle)
            腳色分類 (Role): 傷害輸出 (DPS), 治療 (Heal), 輔助支援 (Support)


*****進階職業(Promotion Classes)*****

    1. 風暴召喚者 輸出  (Stormcaller  DPS)
    2. 大地守護者  治療輔助 (Earthkeeper  Heal/Support)
    3. 大德魯伊  混和 (Archdruid  Hybrid)


*****基本技能(Base Abilites)*****

    **攻擊技能(Attack Abilities)**

        聚焦火花 (Focused Spark)  
            channels lightning at your target for 64 + 80 % Weapon Damage as Electricity Damage
            向目標引導閃電，造成 64 + 80 % 的武器傷害（作為電擊傷害）

    **終極技能 大招(Ultimates)**

        死亡浪潮 (Death Surge)   
            pulls nearby enemies towards you and repeatedly inflicts 40 - 60 +50& Weapon Damage as Nature Damage
            Restores 25% to 35% of your Maximum Health. and gain the Power of Death when the power ends
            Power of Death grants a 10% Damage Bonus for 30 seconds.

            將附近的敵人拉向你並反复造成 40 - 60 +50 和武器傷害作為自然傷害
            恢復最大生命值的 25% 到 35%。
            力量結束時獲得 "死亡之力"
            "死亡之力" 提供 10% 的傷害加成，持續 30 秒。

            You gain a damage preventing barrier and your Expose and Dizzy statuses are removed while casting this power.

            在施展此技能時，你會獲得一個傷害防護屏障，並且你的 "暴露(Expose)" 和 "眩暈(Dizzy)" 狀態會被移除。

            This power can be used while suffering from Control Effects to escape and can be instantly Flash-Cast
            該力量可以在受到 控制效果 的時候使用來逃跑，並且可以 立即順發

    **Retaliations (回復)**

        還擊 (Retaliate) 
            returns you to your feet dealing 88 - 132 + 110% Weapon Damage to nearby enemies and 
            healing you for 3% of your Maximum Health over 3 seconds.

            讓你重新站起來，對附近的敵人造成 88 - 132 + 110% 的武器傷害 並在 3 秒內治療你最大生命值的 3%。

*****基本天賦技能 與 被動  (Base Talent Powers and Passives)*****

    **技能**

        精靈火焰 (Faerie Flames)
            engulfs an enemy in Faerie Flames decreasing Physical Mitigations by 20 for 30 seconds.
            while affected the target cannot stealth.

            用精靈火焰吞沒一名敵人，使其物理減傷降低 20，持續 30 秒。受到影響時目標不能隱身。



        犧牲 (Sacrifice)
            instantly sacrifices 20% of your Maximum Health to restore 20% of your Maximum Essence up to 79% of Maximum

            立即犧牲你最大生命值的 20% 來恢復你最大 精華(Essence) 的 20%  最大值79%



        祭祀 (Ritual Sacrifice)
            instantly sacrifices 8% of your Maximum Health to restore 20% of your Maximum Essence up to 79% of Maximum.

            立即犧牲你最大生命值的 8% 來恢復你最大 精華(Essence) 的 20%  最大值79%



        樹皮皮膚 (Bark Skin )
            toughens the hide of group members and you applying a barrier for 15 seconds
            preventing 280 to 520 damage.

            使團隊成員的皮膚變硬，並且你會施加一個屏障，持續 15 秒，防止 280 到 520 點傷害。




        呼叫風暴 (Call Storm)
            calls lightning to strike a a target stunning them and causing 128 - 192 + 160% Electricity Weapon Damage.

            召喚閃電擊中一個目標，使其昏迷並造成 128 - 192 + 160% 電擊武器傷害。




        自然恩惠 (Nature`s Grace)
            graces you for 12 seconds, healing you for 2% of your Maximum Health each time
            you damage an enemy with a Basic Attack.

            使你恩惠 12 秒，每次你用普通攻擊傷害敵人時，治療你 2% 的最大生命值。




        吸食種子 (Leeching Seed)
            slows and reduces Melee Power Damage Bonus by 20% for 15 seconds.

            減慢 並 降低 近戰傷害加成 20%，持續 15 秒。




        蓋亞之牆 (Gaea`s Wall)
            unleashes a bear spirit that may chain to additional enemies up to two times 
            causing 168 - 252 + 210% Weapon Damage as Nature Damage.

            釋放熊靈，最多可連擊額外的敵人兩次，造成 168 - 252 + 210% 武器傷害（作為自然傷害）。

            Those hit will also suffer 133 - 200 Nature Damage over 12 seconds.

            被擊中的人還會在 12 秒內受到 133 - 200 自然傷害。


    **被動**

        電生成 (Electrogenesis)
            has a chance to spawn Coalesce Life orbs near you when damaging enemies.

            有機率在對敵人造成傷害時在你附近產生 聚結生命球(Coalesce Life orbs)。

    **Powers(技能)**

        復活 (Resurrection )
            targets a small area and revives one unconscious vessel.

            瞄準一小塊區域並喚醒失去知覺的人。



        動力提升 (Kinetic Boost)
            empowers you briefly increasing your Damage Bonus by 20% 
            and Power Efficiency by 20% for 20 seconds.

            使你短暫地增加 20% 的傷害加成和 20% 的能量效率，持續 20 秒。

            empowers you increasing Critical Strike by 15% Critical Damage by 25%
            Damage Bonus by 12% and Attack Power by 175 for 30 seconds.

            to benefit from any form of Avatar for 5 min.

            收到此效果後，您將獲得化身疲勞，並且在 5 分鐘內無法從任何形式的化身中受益。




        閃電爆發 (Lightining Burst)    => 3連續打擊之1 (Combo 1 of 3)

        閃電打擊 (Lightning Strike)   => 3連續打擊之2 (Combo 1 of 2)  
            strike enemies with lightning for 109 - 163 + 136 Weapon Damage as Electricity Damage

            用閃電打擊中敵人，造成 109 - 163 + 136 武器傷害（作為電擊傷害），同時使他們眩暈(Dazing)。



        極光發射器 (Aurora Emitter) => 3連續打擊之3 (Combo 1 of 3)
            strike enemies with lightning in an area for 79 - 119 as Electricity Damage.
            用閃電擊中一個區域內的敵人，造成 79 - 119 點電傷害。

            Enemies damaged are left electrifies and will pulse an additional 27 to 40 + 25%
            Weapon Damage as Electricity Damage over 15 seconds to nearby friendlies.

            受到傷害的敵人會被帶電(electrifies)，並且會在 15 秒內對附近的友軍造成額外的 27 到 40 + 25% 武器傷害
            （作為電擊傷害）。

            if you gain 8 stacks in 10 seconds. Lightining Shield is applied to you.

            如果你在 10 秒內獲得 8 層。獲取 "閃電盾(Lightining Shield)"
